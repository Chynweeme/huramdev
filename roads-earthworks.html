ROADS AND EARTHWORKS

The division of roads and earthworks engages in both conventional and specialized engineering fields. Our flexible and adaptable personnel are knowledgeable with the nuances of conducting business in Africa. We offer unmatched service thanks to our cutting-edge fleet of equipment and knowledgeable logistics crew.

Relying on Huram makes moving to Africa that much simpler.

Sections covered include:
● Bulk earthworks,
● Road construction and rehabilitation,
● Mining infrastructure,
● Construction of HDPE lined containment infrastructure
● Crushing and screening,
● Asphalt manufacture and paving,
● Agricultural land preparation and infrastructural development,
● Water infrastructure construction,
● Rail and rail infrastructure construction,
● Large diameter welded steel pipe installation,
● In situ concrete lining of pipelines, and
● High density polyethylene (HDPE) and ductile cast-iron pipeline installation.
